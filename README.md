# TPRM: Vendor Security Questionnaire Analysis & Risk Assessment
## Project Overview
This repository contains a strategic analysis of a **Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) engagement**, evaluating a vendor's security posture through the lens of a **completed security questionnaire**, identifying high-risk gaps in their control environment, and providing executive-level remediation advice.

## Scenario
A medium-sized organization utilizes a **third-party Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) application** for scientific data analysis. A TPRM questionnaire was sent to them to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the data. Based on the supplier's answers, the main goal is to go over those questions and answers and then summarize the key risk items that should be escalated to the organization's senior management.

## Methodology
The key risk items were summarized by reviewing the questions and answers, doing gap analysis, and determining the risk level of the gaps identified. Key risk domains evaluated include identity and access management, governance, threat detection, operations, and data loss prevention.
